Our Story

In 2010, our founders discovered a rare 1957 jukebox in a dusty old warehouse. This serendipitous find sparked a passion for preserving the golden era of American diners. What started as a simple restoration project evolved into a full-blown love affair with vintage Americana.

Today, Happy Days Diner stands as a testament to that passion. Every detail, from our checkerboard floors to our neon signs, has been carefully curated to transport you back to the fabulous '50s. We're proud to be a family-run establishment, serving up not just delicious food, but a genuine slice of nostalgia.

Our commitment to authenticity extends beyond decor. We source local ingredients whenever possible, ensuring that while our style may be retro, our quality is thoroughly modern. Whether you're a local or just passing through Maryborough, we invite you to step back in time and experience the magic of the '50s with us.
